About IdeaGPT

What is IdeaGPT?

IdeaGPT is an AI-powered idea generator that helps creators, entrepreneurs, and innovators generate unique and creative ideas. Using OpenAI's GPT-3.5 technology, we provide instant inspiration for your next project.

How it Works

Simply click the "Generate Idea" button, and our AI will create a unique, actionable idea for you. Each idea is crafted to be practical and implementable, while maintaining creativity and innovation.

Features

  • Instant idea generation
  • History tracking
  • Dark/light mode
  • Mobile responsive design
